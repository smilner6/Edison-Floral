Edison Floral

Founded in 2024, Edison Floral is a labor of love that was born when its founder designed the flowers for his wedding and the couple decided to put their wedding gifts toward the founding of the business.

With an additional decade of experience in the floral industry and a home-grown flower farm, Edison Floral exists to share the beauty of floral artistry with the greater Detroit community.

Farm in Boston Edison

Our backyard farm has grown since its inception in 2018 and has come to be a source of inspiration in daily life.

Blooms are incorporated into our farmers market boquets, everyday floral arrangements, and as a dealer's choice option in our wedding design.
